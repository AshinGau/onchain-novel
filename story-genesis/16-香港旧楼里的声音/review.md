**Round 1 Issues Identified:**
1. Characters need more distinctive voice and emotional depth
2. The sympathy/empathy theme needs earlier seeds -- protagonist should recognize her loneliness mirrors the ghost's much sooner
3. Ch3 climax needs better pacing -- slow down the horror reveal, add more sensory restraint
4. Need more Hong Kong-specific sensory details (smells, heat, humidity, specific foods)
5. Metaphors should be more unique to this narrator's personality
6. The final section needs more emotional resonance

### Summary

The story passed scoring thresholds after Round 2 (avg 8.125) and was further polished in Round 3 (avg 8.375). Key strengths: the emotional inversion of horror (ghost fears abandonment), the structural sound motif across chapters, and authentic Hong Kong tong lau atmosphere. The protagonist 苏敏's personal loneliness mirrors the ghost Zhang Yulan's trapped state, creating a horror story that functions simultaneously as a meditation on isolation and the human need to be heard.

**Final scores: all dimensions >= 7, average = 8.375. PASS.**
