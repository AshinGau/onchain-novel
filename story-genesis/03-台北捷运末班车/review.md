# 台北捷运末班车 - Genesis Review

## Story Summary
- **Narrator**: Lin Yanting (林彦廷), MRT station attendant at Xiangshan station, 11-year veteran, suffers from micro-blackouts
- **Core Mystery**: Five passengers board the last train, but six arrive at the terminus. The extra passenger shares a name with a woman who died on the tracks three years ago, on the same date, at the same station she claims to have missed.
- **Techniques Used**: Unreliable narrator (blackouts, self-written notes he doesn't remember), closed-space mystery (last train), false and true clues planted throughout

## Open Threads for Co-Authors

1. **The Address** - 信义路三段147巷9号3楼, every Friday at 23:30. What happens there?
2. **The 47 Missing Minutes** - What did Lin Yanting do on November 3rd? Why were pages torn from his notebook?
3. **The Red Thread** - Appears on both Zhou Yiting's wrist and Lin's wrist. What does it signify?
4. **The Couple** - What did the boyfriend pass to the girlfriend? Why has she no exit record? Why was the backpack fuller on arrival?
5. **He Xiuying (Old Lady)** - She received a letter three years ago. She says "not her" involuntarily. What does she know?
6. **Chen Bohan (Suit Man)** - Why does he photograph the staff contact board? Why did he look at Zhou Yiting's seat before she appeared?
7. **The Doppelganger** - Who is the seventh person who looks like Lin? Is it Lin from another time?
8. **The Cover-Up** - Who at the Operations Control Center already knew about the red coat? Why suppress the records?
9. **The Shoes** - Black heels left on the seat like a ritual. The note inside them is unread.
10. **The Crying Sound** - From beneath the train car. What is under there?
