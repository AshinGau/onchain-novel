**Round 1 Issues Identified:**
1. Chapter 1 opening needs a stronger hook — start with a dramatic image, not generic fog
2. Sect exposition dump must be broken up — use dialogue/action to convey information
3. Shen Yanzhou needs distinctive personality traits beyond "talented orphan"
4. Add specific Sichuan sensory details (food, dialect, local customs, flora)
5. Mother's character needs earlier foreshadowing of hidden depth
6. Add moral ambiguity — the revenge path shouldn't be clear-cut
7. Cut cliches, add distinctive metaphors

## Final Notes

**Strengths:**
- The "三剑五寨一座观" faction system is rich and expandable — each faction has clear identity, geography, and political motivation
- Shen Yanzhou avoids the "perfect chosen one" trap through genuine moral doubt and limited combat ability
- The sword philosophy ("听/御/藏", the twelve characters, "洗" vs. "截") provides a deep martial arts framework
- Multiple layers of mystery (murder, conspiracy, missing persons, hidden identities) create sustained tension
- Strong Sichuan atmosphere through specific sensory details rather than generic "misty mountains"
