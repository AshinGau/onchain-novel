## Summary

Three chapters (~22,000 characters total) establishing a modern-day Southeast Asian dark fantasy. The story follows Chen Jiawei, a Singaporean engineer, as he enters the world of 降头术 to save his mother from a飞头降 curse. Key innovations include the "root-line" (根线) magic system with costs tied to memory/sensation loss, the banyan-tree model of master-disciple transmission, and the reveal that Jiawei may be a天生的"器" — a natural vessel being drawn into a larger conspiracy. The story is set for continuation with multiple unresolved threads.
