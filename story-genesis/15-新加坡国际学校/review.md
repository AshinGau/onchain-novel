### Round 2: Character, Scene & Tension Revision

**Changes Made:**
- **Character**: Added Lin Zhiyuan's physical signature — thumb rubbing index fingernail under desk when nervous. Appears in Ch1 classroom scene, Ch2 canteen observation, Ch3 confrontation with Sun Hao. Added Marcus's phone call moments — mysterious calls that make his expression shift, suggesting family pressure beneath the confident surface. Added Sun Hao — a Chinese student who bridges the "Chinese circle" and the Others group, creating internal tension.
- **Scene-based exposition**: Replaced the "three variables" essay section with three embedded scenes: (1) History class — Chen Jiaming told to "rephrase in standard English" after using Singlish, while Marcus's mispronunciation goes uncorrected. (2) Marcus walking through corridors vs. Chinese students' contracted body language. (3) Marcus joking about his C+ grade — the asymmetry of failure costs shown through contrast.
- **Active protagonist**: Added football scene in Ch2 — Lin Zhiyuan uses 40 minutes of bench observation to read Marcus's movement patterns, then intercepts a pass through calculation, not force. This establishes his analytical ability as an active tool, not passive recording.
- **Race tension**: Added "go back to China" canteen incident — a local Chinese student says it to a PRC student, a Malay girl overhears and leaves silently because she's heard the same sentence aimed at herself. The incident shows how racial wounds echo across communities.
- **Marcus complexity**: Added his pause at Daniel Osei's story during International Day — a micro-moment of recognition before he shuts it down. Added Ms. Goh's P.S. revealing Marcus borrowed the class novel after the exhibition.
- **Collaboration hooks**: Added Sun Hao's warning about backlash from Chinese circle. Added Marcus's mysterious phone calls as recurring motif.
- **Metaphor variation**: Replaced several aquarium/fish metaphors with "soft pedal," "rent payment," "chess," and other varied images. Kept fishbowl only for the deliberate prop in Ch3.

### Round 3: Final Polish

**Changes Made:**
- **Ending rewrite**: Replaced the sentimental shadow-merging ending with a scene of sudden tropical rain — Lin and Priya run for cover, the empty fishbowl fills with rain, Priya notes "now it's not empty anymore." Rain filling the fishbowl naturally (from the sky, not from anyone forcing it) is a stronger metaphor than merged shadows.
- **Motif consistency**: Changed the "unknown bird" in Ch1 to a tokay gecko — "嗒嗒嗒" call, specific to Southeast Asia. The gecko reappears in Ch3's final line: "It doesn't need anyone to teach it how to call. It was born knowing." This creates a clean motif: the gecko calls its own name, like the "Others" claiming their own identity.
- **Tropical rain detail**: Added a brief rain passage in Ch1's night scene — "来得突然，走得也快，像一个脾气很大但不记仇的人" — characterizing Singapore weather with personality, echoing the sudden downpour in Ch3.
- **Tightened Priya's "eyes lit up" passage**: Replaced the over-explained aquarium contrast with a single sharp line: "那种亮不是反射来的光——是从里面长出来的。"
- **Final read-through**: Confirmed no logic errors, no timeline inconsistencies, no repeated metaphors, no typos.
